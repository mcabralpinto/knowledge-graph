*List of operations applicable to [[Linear Algebra]] matrices.*
###### **Linear Combination**
Combining a set of vectors by multiplying each vector by a scalar (a real number) and then adding the results together. For example, for vectors $v_1$ and $v_2$​, and scalars $a$ and $b$, the expression $a × v1 + b × v2$ is a linear combination of those vectors.
