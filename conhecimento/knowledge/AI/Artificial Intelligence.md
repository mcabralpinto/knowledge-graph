*Field of Computer Science that explores methods which allow machines to perform tasks that typically require human intelligence, like learning, reasoning and decision-making.*
### Some subfields
- **[[Machine Learning]]**
- **[[Natural Language Processing]]**
- **Computer Vision:** Allows machines to apprehend visual information (images, videos) from the real world as if they were humans.
- **Robotics:** Focus on developing systems that can act in the real world.
