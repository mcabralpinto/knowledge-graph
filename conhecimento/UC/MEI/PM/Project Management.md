### Theory

- **Magic Four**
  - *Quality*
  - *Scope*
  - *Resources*
  - *Time*

- **Risk Assessment** 
  - *Identification* 
  - *Analysis*
  - *Planning* 
  - *Tracking* 
  - *Control*

- **Requirement Engineering** 
  - *Scope Creep* - Happens when requirements aren't well defined. The scope of the project slowly slides away from its original idea, due to the requirements being left to the imagination of the developers.

- **Estimation** 
  - *Uncertainty Cone* - As time goes on, it gets steeper and steeper, meaning we are able to get a closer estimate to reality. This is to be expected: there is less room for unpredictability the more progress is done on something.
