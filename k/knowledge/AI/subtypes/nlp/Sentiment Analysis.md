*The computational treatment of opinions and sentiments (in text, facial expressions, ...), towards more efficient decision making and more empathetic [[Human-AI Interaction]].*
### Tasks
- **Subjectivity Analysis:** Determining whether a piece of text is subjective (expressing personal opinions, feelings, or judgments) or objective (factual information).
- **Polarity Detection:** [[Text Classification|Classifying text]] as positive, negative, or neutral in sentiment.
- **Emotion Recognition:** Identifying specific emotions (e.g., joy, anger, sadness) expressed in the classification target.
- **Intensity Detection:** Measuring the strength or intensity of the expressed sentiment (e.g., very positive, slightly negative).
- **Entity Extraction:** Identifying and extracting entities (people, organizations, locations) mentioned in the text that are associated with specific sentiments.
- **Aspect-based:** Identifying sentiments related to specific aspects or features of a product, service, or entity within the text.
- **Problematic Content Detection:** Detecting hate speech, offensive language, or other forms of problematic content in text.
- **Figurative Language Detection:** Identifying the use of sarcasm, irony, metaphors, and other figurative language that may affect sentiment interpretation.
- **Opinion Properties:** Analyzing additional properties of opinions, such as the source of the opinion, its target, and the time it was expressed.
