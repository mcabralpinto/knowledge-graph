*Also known as NLP, it is a field of AI that uses [[Machine Learning]] to understand and generate human-like language.*

Enabling machines to communicate in natural language is an area that mixes [[Artificial Intelligence|AI]] with linguistics, encompassing a broad range of tasks and requiring knowledge at several levels:
- **Phonetics:** Study of sounds
- **Morphology:** Structure of words
- **Syntax:** Structure of sentences
- **Semantics:** Meaning of words and sentences
- **Pragmatics:** Contextual meaning
- **Discourse:** Structure of texts and conversations

### Tasks
- **[[Text Classification|Classification]]:** Categorizing text into predefined classes (e.g., spam detection, sentiment analysis)
- **Clustering:** Grouping similar documents or text segments together based on [[Distance Metric|similarity]]
- **[[Knowledge Extraction#Sequence Labelling|Sequence Labelling]]:** Assigning labels to each token in a sequence (e.g., chunking, Named Entity Recognition, Part-of-Speech Tagging)
- **Sequence Prediction:** Predicting the next item in a sequence (e.g., language modeling, text generation)
- **Sequence Transduction/Seq2Seq:** Transforming one sequence into another (input $\rightarrow$ output) (e.g., machine translation, summarization)
